About Dataset

This data set contains 891 passengers with 12 variables.

Analysis Section

  • Let's focus on these 3 variables: Pclass, sex and age.
  • Wrangle data from 'titanic_data.csv' to 'titanic.csv' by 'data_wrange.py'.
  • Use excel to remove index from 'titanic.csv'.
  • Create new data 'pclass.csv', 'sex.csv' and 'age.csv' by countifs function in excel.
  • Invesigate and visulize date by 'main.js'.

Conclusion

  • Less half of passengers were survived from the Titanic.
  • The survival rate of 1st class passengers was much greater than that of 3rd class passengers.
  • The survival rate of female passengers was much greater than that of male passengers.
  • A higher percentage of children made it through as compared to the adults.
